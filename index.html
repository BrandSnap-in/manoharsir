About

A Teacher Who Changes
How You See the Subject

For over 25 years, Manohar Sir has been the trusted guide for students and parents across Ghaziabad when it comes to CBSE board preparation. His approach is simple: build the concept first, then the confidence, then the marks follow naturally.

From Class 8 foundations to Class 12 board crunch — whether it's the elegance of a Maths proof, the logic of Chemistry reactions, or the systems of Biology — Manohar Sir brings the same patience, clarity, and personalised attention to every student, every batch.

What We Teach

Subjects & Classes

Expert coaching across all core CBSE science subjects for Classes 8 through 12.

Mathematics

Classes 8 – 12

Algebra, Geometry, Trigonometry, Calculus, Statistics — built concept by concept, with speed and accuracy for board exams.

  • Number Systems & Algebra
  • Coordinate Geometry
  • Calculus (Class 11–12)
  • Probability & Statistics

Science

Classes 8 – 10

Physics, Chemistry and Biology taught as one integrated science — strong NCERT foundation with exam-focused practice.

  • Light, Electricity & Motion
  • Chemical Reactions
  • Life Processes & Genetics
  • NCERT & Board Papers

Chemistry

Classes 11 – 12

Physical, Organic and Inorganic Chemistry with a focus on reaction mechanisms, numerical problems, and NCERT mastery.

  • Physical Chemistry & Numericals
  • Organic Reaction Mechanisms
  • Inorganic & P-Block Elements
  • Board + JEE Foundation

Biology

Classes 11 – 12

Cell biology, genetics, human physiology, ecology — systematic chapter coverage with diagram practice and NEET awareness.

  • Cell Biology & Genetics
  • Human Physiology
  • Plant Processes
  • Ecology & Environment
